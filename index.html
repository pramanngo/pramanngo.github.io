Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)

To comply with the POSH Act, organizations must establish an Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) comprising a woman presiding officer, at least two employee members, one external member, and ensuring at least half of the total members are women.

Learn More


Empowering IC Members and Fostering Awareness

Provide comprehensive training to ICC members and organize regular awareness programs for all employees through diverse formats like in-person sessions, webinars, e-learning modules, podcasts, and plays.
